Sergei Medvedev, Ph.D.

My research encompasses the dynamic intersection of digital technologies and politics within the realm of political communication:
the application of multimedia technologies and big data to political campaigns, multimedia forms of political storytelling,
the role of new media and digital technologies as political tools in authoritarian regimes.

I possess expertise in seamlessly incorporating digital technologies into educational settings, crafting educational
mobile and web applications, designing interactive teaching materials, producing high-quality videos, and
creating compelling graphic designs for various multimedia educational platforms.
