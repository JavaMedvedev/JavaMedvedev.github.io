About Me

Hi there! My name is Seryozha Medvedev but you can call me Suji. I'm an EdTech Specialist and Software Developer based in the west of Ireland.

I earned my PhD in Dublin studying multimedia storytelling and have worked for over a decade in marketing and IT. I established and led the marketing function for a regional office of Luxoft, a DXC Technology Company (NYSE: DXC), from the ground up. My research on multimedia branding is now part of the curriculum at the University of Bologna, Italy.

Today, I manage educational technologies at the University of Galway, develop full-stack applications for education and hospitality and create compelling graphic design for web and print. Crafting immersive, user-centric experiences that breathe life into brands is what keeps me inspired.
