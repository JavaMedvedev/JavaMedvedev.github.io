Things to watch out for:

The Irish and English courts take a different approach on the tort of psychiatric injury (nervous shock) with Ireland taking (so far) a less restrictive approach. In any problem question (and in the Irish courts) only use the Irish law. If the Irish law relies on the English law the English law can also be referred to and cited, but lead where possible with the Irish case that cites it before going on to discuss the English caselaw.
