Kelly v Hennessy [1995] 3 IR 253:
The plaintiff’s husband and two daughters went to the airport to collect the plaintiff’s niece. Some time later the niece telephoned the plaintiff and informed her that her husband and daughters had been seriously injured in a road accident. The plaintiff, it is stated, went into shock and was vomiting and became quite upset. She became further traumatized on visiting the hospital. Ultimately the husband and one daughter suffered brain injuries, the other daughter made a full recovery.
