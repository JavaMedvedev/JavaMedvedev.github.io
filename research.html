Over the course of a decade, my research has focused on the dynamic intersection of digital technologies and politics within the realm of political communication: the application of multimedia technologies and big data to political campaigns, multimedia forms of political storytelling, the role of new media and digital technologies as political tools in authoritarian regimes.
