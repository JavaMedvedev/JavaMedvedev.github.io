RESEARCH

I have a PhD in Media & Communications (Technological University Dublin, 2021), an MA in Media Production and a BA in Journalism.
My research interests span the impact of technological advancements - in media communication and beyond - on human rights from historical and contemporary perspectives, the application of multimedia technologies and big data to social change campaigns, the history and philosophy of science.
My articles and conference papers take stock of the role of ICT in the democratic process, multimedia technologies and big data in anti-corruption and human rights campaigns, methods for analysing political campaigns.
