What is Canvas?

Canvas is the University’s Virtual Learning Environment (VLE). The VLE provides a medium for supporting teaching and learning activities, including assessment and examination.

The Canvas VLE Service Owner is the Centre for Excellence in Learning and Teaching (CELT).

What is the service provided by Information Solutions and Services?

Information Solutions and Services provides technical support for Canvas, including troubleshooting access issues, managing course content, and integrating additional tools like plagiarism detection software.

Canvas Support

Canvas support is available 24/7 via phone or live chat. You can access support through the Help section in Canvas, or by visiting the university’s IT support page for more detailed information.

Related University ICT Policies

The University’s ICT policies govern the use of Canvas and other digital learning tools. Make sure to familiarize yourself with the Acceptable Use Policy, Data Protection Policy, and any other relevant guidelines to ensure compliance.
