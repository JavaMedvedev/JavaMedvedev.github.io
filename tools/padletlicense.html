Padlet Now Available to University of Galway Staff

Padlet Licence for 2024-2025

We are excited to announce that the Padlet tool is fully licensed for the academic year 2024-2025 and is available to all University of Galway staff members. This tool offers powerful, user-friendly collaboration features that can enhance classroom interaction, team projects, and more.

Padlet allows you to easily share ideas, resources, and feedback in real-time, making it an excellent resource for enhancing both in-person and online learning environments.

What is Padlet?

Padlet is an intuitive, cloud-based digital canvas that allows users to create virtual boards for sharing content. Whether you want to brainstorm ideas, gather feedback, or facilitate group discussions, Padlet offers a visually appealing and interactive space where everyone can contribute effortlessly.

You can easily add text, images, videos, links, and more to your Padlet boards. The flexibility of Padlet makes it an excellent tool for collaborative projects, class activities, or as a platform to collect and showcase resources.

Padlet boards can be shared with others through simple links or embedded into websites, making it an extremely versatile tool for fostering interaction among students and staff members.
